Multimodal Pain Management Pathway
Tier 1 (All patients)
  • Acetaminophen 1 g IV/PO q6h (max 4 g/day)
  • Lidocaine 5% patch to chest wall
Tier 2 (Moderate pain)
  • Add NSAID if no contraindication — ketorolac (reduce dose / avoid in elderly or renal impairment; ≤ 5 days)
  • Gabapentin 300 mg PO TID
Tier 3 (Severe / Score ≥ 7)
  • Regional / neuraxial block within 12–24h (epidural, paravertebral, ESP, serratus)
  • PCA if regional contraindicated
  • Minimize IV opioids
▼
SSRF Consult Criteria
  • Flail chest with respiratory failure
  • ≥ 3 severely (bicortically) displaced fractures
  • Failure to wean from mechanical ventilation
  • Symptomatic non-union / malunion with chronic pain
Thoracic surgery consult within 24h
Step-Down / Discharge Criteria
  • Pain controlled on oral regimen
  • FVC ≥ ~50% predicted / adequate spirometry
  • SpO₂ > 92% on room air
  • Adequate cough, no respiratory distress
