# UE Post-Event Huddle Checklist

**Hold huddle within 1 hour of UE event.**

## Participants

- RN present
- RT present
- Covering provider present

## Checklist

1. Confirm immediate patient stabilization completed.
2. Confirm UE event form completion started.
3. Verify sedation target and current RASS documentation.
4. Verify restraint status and integrity review.
5. Verify ETT securement documentation (if reintubated).
6. Identify likely primary cause category.
7. Assign one immediate process fix before end of shift.
8. Confirm event entered into UE tracker.
