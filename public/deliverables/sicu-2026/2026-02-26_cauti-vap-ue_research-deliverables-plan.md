# CAUTI / VAP / UE Research and Deliverables Plan

**Prepared:** February 26, 2026  
**Scope:** SICU quality deliverables for committee execution and PI tracking

## Program Goal

Create a single practical workplan that converts current discussions into measurable CAUTI, VAP, and UE improvements over 90 days.

## Deliverables by Workstream

## 1) CAUTI Workstream

### Deliverables

1. Final trauma/SICU Foley guideline (committee-approved version).
2. One-page resident/APP bedside quick guide.
3. Urine-culture stewardship trigger card for rounds.
4. CAUTI national benchmark and CMS impact brief for leadership alignment.
5. Monthly CAUTI process dashboard:
- Foley device utilization
- Foley-days with documented indication
- Non-indicated urine cultures
- CAUTI events/SIR

### Data Sources

- EMR order and documentation fields
- Infection prevention event log
- Unit audit sampling

## 2) VAP Workstream

### Deliverables

1. RN/RT oral-care accountability standard (who does what, when).
2. Subglottic ET rollout implementation tracker.
3. SAT/SBT compliance tracker (time-start and completion reason).
4. Monthly VAP/vent-day dashboard with baseline and trend.

### Data Sources

- RT logs and vent-day counts
- EMR task completion data
- Quality reporting extracts

## 3) UE Workstream

### Deliverables

1. One-page UE event form (RN + RT + provider completion in 1 hour).
2. UE event tracker (rate per 100 ventilator days).
3. Post-event huddle checklist.
4. Monthly UE review section in SICU PI meeting template.

### Data Sources

- Bedside UE forms
- RT ventilator day denominator
- PI committee logs

## 4) Case Review Standardization

### Deliverables

1. Case-closure template language for SICU minutes.
2. Event-classification checklist (CAUTI/HAP/VAP/other) to reduce mislabeling.
3. Required-elements checklist for self-extubation documentation.

## Owner Matrix (Assign Named Person at Next Meeting)

|---|---|---|
| CAUTI guideline + stewardship tools | SICU Director / APP Lead | Infection Prevention |

## Success Metrics (Initial Targets)

1. >=90% Foley-days with documented indication.
2. >=30% reduction in non-indicated urine cultures.
3. >=85% documented oral-care interval compliance.
4. >=85% SAT/SBT documentation compliance before 08:00.
5. UE events tracked at 100% capture with documented post-event huddle.

## Meeting Cadence for This Plan

- Weekly 15-minute operational huddle (execution blockers only).
- Monthly SICU committee review (metrics + decisions).
- Quarterly PI summary (trend, outcomes, resource requests).
