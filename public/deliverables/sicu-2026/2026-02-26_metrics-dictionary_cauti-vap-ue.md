# CAUTI/VAP/UE Metrics Dictionary (Operational)

## Purpose

Standardize metric names, definitions, and numerator/denominator logic used in monthly SICU reporting.

## CAUTI Metrics

### 1) Foley Device Utilization Ratio

- Definition: catheter-days / patient-days
- Source: nursing device day counts
- Frequency: monthly

### 2) Foley-Days with Documented Indication

- Definition: percent of Foley-days with explicit indication documentation
- Numerator: Foley-days with documented indication
- Denominator: total Foley-days

### 3) Non-Indicated Urine Culture Rate

- Definition: urine cultures ordered without documented qualifying indication
- Numerator: non-indicated cultures
- Denominator: total urine cultures ordered in SICU

## VAP Process Metrics

### 4) Oral-Care Interval Compliance

- Definition: percent of required oral-care intervals completed/documented
- Numerator: completed required intervals
- Denominator: scheduled required intervals

### 5) SAT/SBT Early Compliance

- Definition: percent of eligible vented patients with SAT/SBT completed (or contraindication documented) by 08:00
- Numerator: eligible patients meeting standard
- Denominator: all eligible vented patients

### 6) Subglottic ET Utilization (Eligible Cases)

- Definition: percent of eligible intubations using subglottic-capable ET tubes
- Numerator: eligible intubations with subglottic tube
- Denominator: all eligible intubations

## UE Metrics

### 7) UE Event Rate per 100 Ventilator Days

- Definition: (UE count / vent days) x 100
- Numerator: number of UE events in period
- Denominator: total vent days in period

### 8) UE Form Completion Compliance

- Definition: percent of UE events with complete RN+RT+provider form within 1 hour
- Numerator: UE events with complete form on time
- Denominator: total UE events

### 9) UE Huddle Compliance

- Definition: percent of UE events with documented bedside huddle within 1 hour
- Numerator: UE events with timely huddle
- Denominator: total UE events
